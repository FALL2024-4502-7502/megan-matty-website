The Matty-Murray Olympics of 2024

Every 4th of July my family hosts our own Olympics. These games range from the normal water balloon toss and eating competition These games also include some family favorites like: who can spit a cherry pit the farthest and who can leash Larry the dog the quickest. Games are played. Memories are made. And good times are had by all.
